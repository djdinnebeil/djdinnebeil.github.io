About Me

Hello, I am DJ. I’m a software engineer with a strong interest in Python, Windows development, and the foundational concepts of programming languages. When I’m not coding, I enjoy hiking, discovering new trails, and spending time with my cat, Tabby, who keeps me company during late-night projects.

My Journey

My journey into software engineering has been a blend of academic rigor, military discipline, and a deep curiosity for solving complex problems. It began at the University of Delaware, where I first explored programming through courses in C and C++, laying the foundation for my technical journey.

While attending college, I joined the United States Coast Guard Reserves and served as an Electronics Technician. During this time, I developed a passion for mathematics and philosophy, which led me to earn a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and a Master of Arts in American Studies. These experiences shaped my analytical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

Despite my academic achievements, I realized my true career aspirations lay in programming and technology. To pursue this path, I returned to college and earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Stockton University. To further refine my skills, I completed App Academy’s intensive 1000+ hour software engineering program, gaining practical experience in full-stack development and advanced programming concepts.

As someone whose primary operating system is Windows, I have a strong interest in mastering C++, which is Microsoft’s recommended language for Windows application development. This led to the creation of Auto Core, a C++ utility designed to streamline task execution by remapping the numpad keys to user-defined commands.

Through this journey, I have developed a solid foundation in software engineering, driven by a commitment to technical excellence and continuous learning. I am excited to bring this perspective to solve meaningful problems and build innovative projects.
